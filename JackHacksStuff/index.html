About

I'm a Dublin-based penetration tester working toward my first professional security role. I hold a Level 7 degree in Digital Forensics & Cybersecurity from TU Dublin, a Google Professional Cybersecurity Certificate, and recently passed the eJPT — eLearnSecurity Junior Penetration Tester certification.

This portfolio is where I document my hands-on work — TryHackMe CTF rooms, lab environments, and detection engineering challenges. I write up everything: the paths that worked, the ones that didn't, and what I learned from both. I think the dead ends are as useful to document as the flags.

My offensive work covers web exploitation, Active Directory, cryptography failures, and CVE-based exploitation. The blue-team side includes Sigma rule authoring, Sysmon-based detection engineering, and SIEM log analysis with Splunk and Wazuh.
